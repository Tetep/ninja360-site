## Where to grab each link

### Kuula (most of your 360 tours)
1. Go to kuula.co and log in.
2. Open the tour or collection.
3. Click the "Share" button (top right).
4. Copy the link. It looks like:  https://kuula.co/share/collection/XXXXX
   or  https://kuula.co/share/XXXXX

### Matterport
1. Go to my.matterport.com and log in.
2. Open the space.
3. Click "Share" -> copy link. It looks like:
   https://my.matterport.com/show/?m=XXXXX

### Your own host (virtualtour.ninja-360.com)
- Open the tour and copy the URL from the address bar
  (looks like https://virtualtour.ninja-360.com/share/collection/XXXX).

### YouTube (videos / interviews)
1. Go to youtube.com/@ninja360vr -> Videos.
2. Open the video, click "Share", copy the link
   (or just copy the watch URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XXXXX).

### Drone photos
- For now just say "have drone photos" - we'll upload those to GHL media later.
